Child Experience

What your child sees & does

SproutPath's child interface is calm, colourful, and completely distraction-free — designed so children can navigate independently with confidence.

Dashboard

A calm home base built just for them

When your child opens SproutPath, they're greeted by their name, photo, and today's star count. Quick-access tiles give instant entry to every corner of the app — no menus, no confusion.

  • ⚡ Up to 8 customisable Quick Access tiles, reorderable by parent or child
  • ⭐ Live star chart — today's earned stars shown at a glance
  • 📸 My Memories strip — a horizontal gallery of saved moments
  • 👤 Personal profile card with photo avatar, name, age & favourite colour
Emotions

Understanding feelings, step by step

The Emotions module helps children recognise, name, and respond to their own feelings and others'. Built around evidence-based methods used in autism therapy.

  • 😊 Daily Emotion Check-in — visual picker to log how they feel
  • 🪞 Mirror Mode — front camera so children practise expressions safely
  • ✨ Identify Emotions AI — camera detects facial expressions in real time
  • 📚 Social Stories Library — pre-built stories for school, sharing & change
  • ⏱️ 6-Second Rule — guided breathing pause before reacting to big feelings
  • ✍️ My Stories (Custom) — personalised stories created by parents
  • 💭 Worries Box — a private journal for logging fears and anxieties
Study

Learning at their pace, in their language

Study is organised into two clear groups — Numbers & Logic and Language & Stories — so children can work through subjects at a manageable pace. All content adapts to the app's active language.

  • 🔢 Math & Numbers — counting, addition, subtraction, multiplication tables
  • 📏 Measurements — length, weight, volume, and time with visual aids
  • 💰 Currency & Kiddy Bank — learn money with an interactive bank simulation
  • 📖 English Lessons — alphabet, phonics, word recognition & sentence-building
  • 🔤 Mother Tongue — reading and vocabulary in Telugu, Hindi, Tamil & 9 more
  • 🔬 Science Discovery — age-appropriate concepts with illustrations
  • 🎤 Speaking Practice — guided prompts for verbal communication
  • 🎴 Flashcards — vocabulary, sight words & concepts as flip-card drills
Activities

Move, create, and find their calm

Activities is grouped into three clear categories so children always know where to find what they need — whether they want to move, create, or simply wind down.

  • 🧘 Yoga & Exercise Videos — child-friendly sequences and physical guides
  • 🌬️ Calm Corner — breathing animations, calming sounds & grounding exercises
  • 🖍️ Colouring Canvas — digital pages to fill with fingers or stylus
  • 🍳 Cooking Videos — simple, safe activities demonstrated step by step
  • ⚽ Sports & Physical Activities — cricket, swimming, badminton, skating & more
  • 🙏 Devotional — content in 9 Indian languages for spiritual routines
  • 🎴 Flashcard Learning Games — vocabulary, shapes, colours & concepts
Schedule

Predictable days that reduce anxiety

A visual, time-blocked schedule lets children see exactly what comes next. Tapping a task marks it complete, building a sense of accomplishment throughout the day.

  • 📅 Visual Day Timeline — time-blocked tasks with icons in order
  • ✅ Tap-to-Complete Checkboxes — children check off tasks themselves
  • 🗓️ Weekly View — swipe through the week to see upcoming days
  • 🪔 Festivals Calendar — cultural occasions appear automatically
  • 🔔 Schedule Notifications — gentle reminders so nothing is missed
  • 📋 Daily Routine Checklist — separate morning & evening checklists
  • 🔀 First-Then Board — visual prompt showing what's next after a task
  • 🎯 Choice Board — let children pick between approved options
Videos & Explore

See the world from the safety of home

The Videos tab and Explore section give children a rich, safe window into learning through visual content — from skill-building demonstrations to virtual world tours.

  • 🎬 Educational Video Library — curated across yoga, social skills, cooking & more
  • 🌍 Virtual Tours — explore world landmarks, monuments & museums
  • 🏛️ Places Explorer — discover zoos, parks & cultural sites
  • 🎭 Video Modeling — watch children demonstrate daily life skills step by step
  • 🗺️ Kids Navigation (Singapore) — child-friendly map of nearby safe spaces
Wellbeing

Calm tools for big feelings

When emotions run high, the Calm Corner gives children a soft landing — guided techniques, visual tools, and a rewards system that makes progress visible and rewarding.

  • 🌬️ Calm Corner — breathing animations, calming sounds & sensory exercises
  • ⏱️ Visual Timer — a colour-draining circle shows time passing
  • ⭐ Stars & Rewards — earn stars for completing tasks; view the star chart
  • 📷 My Memories — capture and save photos of happy moments
  • 💬 AAC Communication Board — symbol board for non-verbal expression
  • 🎙️ Voice Chat Assistant — conversational AI for thoughts children can't verbalise

Parent Experience

Everything parents and caregivers control

The parent side of SproutPath is a protected, comprehensive hub for monitoring, configuring, and supporting your child's journey — PIN-locked and completely private.

Parents Hub

One protected space for everything parent-related

The Parents Hub is a dedicated PIN-locked section giving caregivers full visibility and control — progress tracking, content management, child safety, and specialist resources, all in one place.

  • 👨‍👩‍👧 Progress Summary Dashboard — see which sections your child has visited
  • 👤 Multi-Profile Management — add, switch & manage a profile per child
  • 🔒 PIN-Protected Parent Mode — 4-digit passcode gates all adult tools
  • 📸 Family Photo Banner — upload a family photo as the hub banner
  • 📝 Handoff Notes — leave notes for other caregivers between sessions
  • 🎯 Goal-Setting — set and track developmental milestones per child
Behaviour Journal

Spot patterns before they become crises

Log difficult moments with trigger, severity, resolution, location and duration. After two or more weeks, a pattern heatmap reveals the time-of-day and day-of-week trends — invaluable for therapists and carers.

  • 📝 Detailed Entry Logging — trigger, severity (1–5), resolution, location & duration
  • 🔥 Pattern Heatmap — after 2+ weeks, visualise highest-risk days & times
  • 📊 Severity Colour Coding — entries colour-coded green → red at a glance
  • 🔒 On-Device Only — journal entries never uploaded or shared
Emergency Card

Critical information, ready when it matters most

A one-screen card parents can show to teachers, doctors, strangers, and emergency responders. Exportable as an image or PDF so it's always within reach.

  • 👦 Child Identity Info — name, age, diagnosis & photo in one glanceable format
  • 💬 Communication Style — explain how your child communicates to responders
  • 😌 Calming Strategies & Do-Nots — critical for de-escalation in public
  • 📞 2 Emergency Contacts + Doctor — primary, backup & medical professional
  • 📤 Export as Image or PDF — printable for school bags and wallets
Sensory Profile

Document your child's sensory world

Map your child's sensory sensitivities across 7 senses on a 3-point scale. The resulting visual profile card can be shared with teachers, therapists, and carers instantly.

  • 🗺️ 7-Sense Assessment — sound, touch, sight, smell, taste, movement & body awareness
  • 📊 3-Point Scale — rate each sense as Sensitive, Neutral, or Seeking
  • 📤 Shareable Profile Card — screenshot and share instantly
  • ✏️ Free-Text Notes Per Sense — add specific examples beyond the rating
My People & AAC

Build a world of familiar, recognisable faces

Parents upload photos of family members and label them. Children can then use the camera to identify who is near them — a calming, familiar anchor in new situations.

  • 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Family Faces Gallery — upload & label family, friends, teachers & carers
  • 📷 Live Face Identification — point the camera to identify the person
  • 🗂️ AAC Symbol Board Builder — add, edit & organise communication symbols
  • ✏️ Custom Social Stories Creator — build stories with the child's name & scenarios
Settings & Security

Full control in the parent's hands

Every setting that affects the child experience is controlled by the parent. From language and theme to audio preferences and app-locking, parents configure once and the child experiences stability.

  • 🔒 PIN-Locked Parent Mode — tap "Parent Mode" and enter your PIN
  • 🔑 Guided Access Integration — pairs with iOS to prevent children leaving the app
  • 🌐 Language Switching — change the app language for the whole family instantly
  • 🌙 Display Themes — light, dark, and high-contrast modes
  • 🔊 Voice & Audio Controls — toggle text-to-speech & background audio independently
  • 🔔 Schedule Notifications — configure time slots for gentle daily reminders

Frequently asked questions

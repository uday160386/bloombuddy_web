Common questions

Everything parents ask

Is BloomBuddy free to download? ▾
Yes — BloomBuddy is free to download on the App Store and Google Play. Core features are available immediately with no subscription required.
Does BloomBuddy collect my child's personal information? ▾
Absolutely not. All data stays only on your device. Nothing is sent anywhere — this is guaranteed by how the app is built, not just a policy.
Can I use BloomBuddy in Hindi or my regional language? ▾
Yes! BloomBuddy supports 12 languages including Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Bengali, Marathi, Gujarati, Kannada, Malayalam, Punjabi, Odia, Assamese and English. Switch in one tap.
Does the app work without internet? ▾
Yes. Core features — communication board, schedules, calming tools, emotion learning — work fully offline. Internet is only needed for video streaming and YouTube social stories.
Is BloomBuddy suitable for different ages? ▾
BloomBuddy is designed for children broadly aged 3 to 14. Parents can personalise every aspect — symbols, routines, reward system — to suit their child's current abilities. The app grows with your child.
Can multiple children in my family use BloomBuddy? ▾
Yes. BloomBuddy supports multiple child profiles on a single device. Each child has their own settings, communication board, progress and rewards.
